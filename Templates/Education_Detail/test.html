In a Basic Qur'an class, participants focus on improving their Qur'an recitation by mastering Tajweed rules, such as proper articulation of letters (Makharij al-Huruf), understanding the characteristics of sounds (Sifat al-Huruf), and applying elongation rules (Madd). The class typically involves guided recitation practice, where students recite individually or in groups while receiving corrections from the teacher. By the end of the class, students aim to recite beautifully, accurately, and confidently, connecting more deeply with the Qur'an.
